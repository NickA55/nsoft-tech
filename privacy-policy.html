W.I.M.I. (Will I Make It)

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026

W.I.M.I. (“Will I Make It”) is a bill tracking app designed to help users track bills, amounts, and due dates.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information You Provide

W.I.M.I. allows you to enter information such as:

  • bill names
  • payment amounts
  • due dates

All information is stored locally on your device. It is not transmitted to any server controlled by the developer.

No Accounts or Personal Data Collection

  • No account creation required
  • No personal data collected
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising services used

Subscriptions and Purchases

W.I.M.I. uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions.

When you make or restore a purchase:

  • purchase-related data may be processed to validate transactions
  • subscription status is managed to enable premium features

Payment information is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play. W.I.M.I. does not store payment information.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored only on your device
  • No cloud sync is provided
  • Data may be removed when the app is uninstalled (subject to device backup settings)

Data Sharing

W.I.M.I. does not sell or share your information. The only exception is limited data required to process purchases through platform providers and RevenueCat.

Security

Data security depends on your device’s protections. No method of storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

W.I.M.I. is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.
